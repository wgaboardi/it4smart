Introduction

This is a site dedicated to IT professionals who are curious like me and love to invest their time to get to know new technologies, frameworks, or even read interesting content, whether simply to stay more updated or even to know and apply it at work or at home.

My name is Wellington L Gaboardi. I'm from Brazil. I've been working in IT for over 30 years and my favorite hobby is studying (besides applying everything at work, of course!!). With a background in Data Processing, with two postgraduate degrees in Computer Networks and IT Governance respectively, and currently finishing another degree in Accounting Sciences, I have already worked at prominent companies in Brazil, in the area of Human Resources, Education, Health and currently work in the technology area of the Federal Government for over 17 years.
